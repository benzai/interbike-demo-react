Shape Up

Icon design / Illustration

ShapeUp is a social health platform that helps employees to improve their health through the power of social networks and their peers support. I worked together with the design team to create a new sleek and colorful look. My job was to create several sets of icons and illustrations that would improve user experience and add fun to the serious health issues.
